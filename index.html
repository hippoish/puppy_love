    All About Gilda

  • Beautiful strawberry-blond hair that requires daily brushing
  • Deep, soulful puppy dog eyes and a great smile
  • Ready for romance
  • Proud single mother of five adorable, bandana-wearing, leash trained puppies (Does it count as being a single parent if the owner takes care of all of them?!)

    Gilda is looking for a soulmate who...

  • Is well trained and well behaved
  • Isn't selfish about sharing his tennis ball in a game of fetch
  • Is patient and kind to puppies and humans alike

Gilda's Perfect Date

Gilda loves nothing more than a long walk to the park, followed by hours of playing fetch at the park, followed by a good chunk of time spent chewing her bed/toys/the occassional couch cushion to shreds, followed by an afternoon-long nap on a cool tile floor.

If you don't mind excessive shedding and a bit of slobbering, and are looking for an eternally good natured partner, Gilda is the one for you!

Gilda's Deal Breakers

Barkers, biters, growlers, jumpers, and shoe chewers need not apply!
